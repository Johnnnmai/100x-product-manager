# prioritize

Use this when several initiatives all sound important and someone needs to decide what happens now, later, or never.

## Input

- candidate initiatives or features
- current constraints
- objective or business context
- resource limits

## Output

- recommendation
- explicit trade-offs
- what to defer
- downside of the chosen path
- stakeholder impact
- next review checkpoint

## Default Workflow

1. Clarify the decision type with `prioritization-advisor`
2. Bring in market or business pressure if needed with `market-driven-prioritization`
3. Compare upside vs cost with `feature-investment-advisor`
4. If pricing or revenue trade-offs matter, use `finance-based-pricing-advisor`

## Operating Rules

- Force an order, not a brainstorm.
- Explicitly name what loses when something wins.
- Make downside visible.
- Prefer reversible sequencing over false certainty.

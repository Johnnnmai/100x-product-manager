# Design Eval Summary

## Result

`design-eval` produced the biggest gain in the pilot.

## Why 100X PM Won

- it turned a vague test plan into launch logic
- it required pass/fail criteria instead of generic QA advice
- it made human review and fallback part of the output

## Why Baseline Lost

- it named categories but not operating thresholds
- it did not define what should block launch
- it left too much ambiguity around medium-high trust risk

## Caveat

This still needs a proper eval dataset and multiple reviewers. But even in pilot form, the command made the artifact operational.

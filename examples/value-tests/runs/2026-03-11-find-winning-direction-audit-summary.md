# Find Winning Direction Audit Summary

## Result

`find-winning-direction` in product audit mode beat the plain prompt on both quality and efficiency.

## Why 100X PM Won

- it converted product feedback into an explicit wedge decision
- it tied the recommendation to trusted workflow behavior, not generic feature advice
- it made the business model and channel implication visible
- it ended with a real execution path instead of a loose suggestion list

## Why Baseline Lost

- it stayed at "improve the product" altitude
- it never picked a strategic story
- it treated all problems as equal instead of choosing the highest-leverage move

## Caveat

This was still an internal pilot with one operator and no blinded review.
It is useful as front-door proof, not as final external evidence.

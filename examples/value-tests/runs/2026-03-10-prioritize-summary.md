# Prioritize Summary

## Result

`prioritize` beat the plain prompt, mostly because it forced explicit losers and review rules.

## Why 100X PM Won

- it turned the problem into a decision against a goal
- it made it clear what gets delayed and why
- it surfaced the downside of the chosen path

## Why Baseline Lost

- it produced a ranking without a decision frame
- it did not show what the team was consciously sacrificing
- it left too much work for the human reviewer

## Caveat

A stronger future version should add cost and effort estimates, but even this pilot was materially more actionable.

# 2026-03-11 Product Audit Input Bundle

## Scenario

Audit an existing B2B SaaS product and decide what the strongest product bet should be.

## Website Snapshot

- URL: `https://pulsepilot.example`
- Category: AI customer-success copilot
- Landing page promise:
  - "Turn every customer call into risk scores, follow-up actions, and renewal signals"
  - "AI writes the next-step email for your CSM"
  - "Spot churn before it is too late"

## Public Signal Bundle

### Review-style signals

1. "Summaries are helpful, but the churn score feels made up."
2. "The alerts created more noise than signal."
3. "Great on single calls, weak on account context."
4. "CSMs copied the summary into Slack, but rarely trusted the risk number."
5. "Setup felt heavy for teams without clean CRM data."

### Support / operator notes

1. Pilot users loved the concise recap after calls.
2. Teams wanted next-step guidance more than another dashboard.
3. Managers asked for proof that the score correlates with actual renewals.
4. Sales liked the story, but customer success wanted workflow compression, not more analysis.

## Constraints

- 2 engineers
- 1 designer
- 8 weeks for a meaningful proof point
- no CRM writeback in v1
- must show value inside a weekly workflow

## Success Criteria

- find the strongest product audit insight
- recommend a sharper wedge
- make the business model and channel implications explicit
- define the next execution step

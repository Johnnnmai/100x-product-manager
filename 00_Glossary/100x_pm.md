# 100X PM Glossary / 100X 产品经理术语表

[English](#english) | [中文](#中文)

---

## English

### Core Concepts

#### 10X PM
**Definition:** Individual productivity gains - working faster and better than peers.

**Focus:** Personal efficiency, speed, individual output

**Example:** Writing PRDs in half the time, shipping features faster

---

#### 100X PM
**Definition:** Organizational leverage - changing the output curve of a team or organization through systems.

**Focus:** Mechanisms, platforms, decision quality, risk containment, scaled delivery

**Example:** Building a system that makes 10 engineers 10x more productive

---

### Key Terms

#### MECE
**Definition:** Mutually Exclusive, Collectively Exhaustive - a categorization framework where items don't overlap and cover all possibilities.

**Usage:** Used in skill card categorization to ensure complete coverage without redundancy.

---

#### Skill Card
**Definition:** A reusable PM decision framework with defined inputs, outputs, pass criteria, and context adapters.

**Components:**
- Definition
- When to use
- Outputs (artifacts)
- Pass criteria
- Red flags
- Example
- Context Adapter (Startup vs Big Tech)

---

#### Artifact
**Definition:** A concrete deliverable produced by executing a skill card.

**Examples:**
- Problem Statement
- Metrics Tree
- Experiment Design
- Launch Checklist
- Stakeholder Map

---

#### Playbook
**Definition:** An ordered sequence of skill cards designed for a specific scenario.

**Examples:**
- startup_mvp_0_to_1
- bigtech_feature_launch
- regulated_platform_change

---

#### Context Adapter
**Definition:** A section in each skill card that defines different requirements for Startup vs Big Tech contexts.

**Purpose:** Ensures the same skill is applicable across different organizational contexts.

---

#### Pass Criteria
**Definition:** The minimum standards that must be met for a skill execution to be considered successful.

**Importance:** Provides objective metrics to evaluate skill execution quality.

---

#### Red Flags
**Definition:** Warning signs that indicate a skill is being executed incorrectly or improperly.

**Purpose:** Helps identify and prevent common mistakes.

---

## 中文

### 核心概念

#### 10X PM
**定义:** 个人生产力提升——比同龄人更快、更好地工作。

**重点:** 个人效率、速度、个人产出

**例子:** 用一半时间写PRD、更快地发布功能

---

#### 100X PM
**定义:** 组织杠杆——通过系统改变团队或组织的产出曲线。

**重点:** 机制、平台、决策质量、风险管控、规模化交付

**例子:** 构建一个让10个工程师效率提高10倍的系统

---

### 关键术语

#### MECE
**定义:** 相互独立、完全穷尽——一种分类框架，其中项目不重叠且覆盖所有可能性。

**用法:** 用于技能卡分类，确保完整覆盖且无冗余。

---

#### Skill Card
**定义:** 一个可复用的PM决策框架，具有定义的输入、输出、通过标准和上下文适配器。

**组成部分:**
- 定义
- 何时使用
- 输出（产出物）
- 通过标准
- 红旗（常见错误）
- 例子
- 上下文适配器（Startup vs Big Tech）

---

#### Artifact
**定义:** 执行技能卡产生的具体交付物。

**例子:**
- 问题陈述
- 指标树
- 实验设计
- 发布检查清单
- 利益相关方地图

---

#### Playbook
**设计:** 为特定场景设计的技能卡有序序列。

**例子:**
- startup_mvp_0_to_1
- bigtech_feature_launch
- regulated_platform_change

---

#### 上下文适配器
**定义:** 技能卡中定义Startup和Big Tech不同要求的部分。

**目的:** 确保相同的技能适用于不同的组织环境。

---

#### 通过标准
**定义:** 执行技能必须满足的最低标准，才被视为成功。

**重要性:** 提供客观指标来评估技能执行质量。

---

#### 红旗
**定义:** 表明技能执行不正确或不恰当的警告信号。

**目的:** 帮助识别和预防常见错误。

---

## 10X vs 100X Comparison

| Dimension | 10X PM | 100X PM |
|-----------|--------|---------|
| **Focus** | Personal speed | Organizational leverage |
| **Unit of work** | Individual tasks | Systems and processes |
| **Success metric** | Personal output | Team/organization output |
| **Time horizon** | This week, this sprint | This quarter, this year |
| **Key skills** | Execution, speed | Design, governance, alignment |
| **Risk mindset** | Minimize personal risk | Optimize risk/reward at org level |
| **Learning mode** | Learn faster individually | Build learning mechanisms for org |

---

## 10X vs 100X 对比

| 维度 | 10X PM | 100X PM |
|------|--------|---------|
| **重点** | 个人速度 | 组织杠杆 |
| **工作单元** | 个人任务 | 系统和流程 |
| **成功指标** | 个人产出 | 团队/组织产出 |
| **时间跨度** | 本周、本迭代 | 本季度、本年度 |
| **关键技能** | 执行、速度 | 设计、治理、对齐 |
| **风险心态** | 最小化个人风险 | 在组织层面优化风险/回报 |
| **学习模式** | 个人学习更快 | 为组织构建学习机制 |

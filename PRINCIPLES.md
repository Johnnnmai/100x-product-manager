# 100X PM Principles

## What Is 100X PM?

100X PM is not about doing 100x more work.

It is about becoming the kind of PM or founder who can turn ambiguity into decisions, move through aggressive iteration, and use AI plus systems to create outsized outcomes.

The gap between an average PM and a top-tier PM is often not 2x to 5x.
In the moments that matter, it can be 100x in:

- decision quality
- convergence speed
- alignment speed
- wasted work avoided

## 10 Rules

1. **Define the problem before proposing the solution.**
2. **Use evidence, not noise, to validate demand.**
3. **Define success before shipping.**
4. **Make trade-offs explicit.**
5. **AI PM is not prompt writing. It includes evals, fallback, guardrails, and behavior design.**
6. **If execution cost is lower than thinking cost, ship the smallest viable version and learn.**
7. **Aggressive iteration beats polished hesitation.**
8. **Leverage beats effort.**
9. **PRDs must increasingly be readable by both humans and agents.**
10. **The best PMs create non-linear outcomes, not just better documents.**

## What This Means In Practice

- A rough idea should become a wedge, a scope, and a next move quickly.
- Demand should be judged through evidence bundles, not stakeholder volume.
- Specs should support review by humans and execution by agents.
- AI features should not ship without evals, fallback logic, and review triggers.
- When a cheap test can answer the question faster than more internal debate, run the test.

## Four Multipliers

### Judgment

Frame the right problem, choose the right user, and make the real trade-off visible.

### Execution

Turn decisions into sharper specs, cleaner alignment, and faster motion.

### AI Behavior

Define prompts, evals, fallback, constraints, and human handoff.

### Leverage

Use systems, workflows, templates, and agents to produce outsized output quality.

# Problem & Discovery Pack

Validate demand, collect evidence, frame the problem, and turn research into decisions.

## Included skills

- `demand-reality-checker` — Judge whether a demand signal is real, urgent, and worth acting on.
- `evidence-collector` — Normalize interviews, tickets, comments, dashboards, and sales notes into one evidence bundle.
- `evidence-triangulator` — Test whether multiple evidence sources actually support the same conclusion.
- `discovery-interview-prep` — Build a sharper interview plan that generates decision-grade evidence.
- `interview-question-builder` — Generate a better sequence of interview questions and probing prompts.
- `problem-framing-canvas` — Separate symptom, user pain, impact, constraints, and unknowns before proposing solutions.
- `problem-statement-writer` — Write a clean problem statement that can anchor PRDs, memos, and experiments.
- `user-insight-synthesizer` — Turn messy qualitative notes into patterns, tensions, and decisions.
- `competitor-pattern-scan` — Scan competitor moves for patterns without overreacting to noise.
- `signal-vs-noise-analyzer` — Separate high-value recurring signals from low-quality noise.

# PRD & Scope Pack

Move from fuzzy problem to review-ready docs, scoped MVPs, and clearer requirements.

## Included skills

- `prd-coverage-checker` — Scan a PRD for missing logic, missing sections, and weak review readiness.
- `prd-one-pager-writer` — Turn a fuzzy idea into a compact 1-page product brief.
- `acceptance-criteria-writer` — Convert requirements into observable pass/fail acceptance criteria.
- `mvp-scope-cutter` — Cut scope aggressively without cutting the learning loop.
- `requirements-ambiguity-fixer` — Rewrite vague requirement language into clearer, testable statements.
- `release-readiness-check` — Check whether a feature is actually ready to launch across product, engineering, ops, and measurement.
- `decision-log-operator` — Capture key product decisions, trade-offs, and revisit triggers.

# Launch, Growth & Pricing Pack

Prepare launches, adoption motion, activation, and pricing decisions.

## Included skills

- `launch-brief-writer` — Create a launch brief with scope, owners, risks, and measurement.
- `gtm-readiness-check` — Check whether enablement, comms, support, and adoption motion are actually ready.
- `finance-based-pricing-advisor` — Evaluate pricing moves through business math, value capture, and adoption risk.

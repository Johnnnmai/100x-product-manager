# Metrics & Experimentation Pack

Define success, instrument what matters, design experiments, and interpret results.

## Included skills

- `success-metric-spec-writer` — Define primary metric, support metrics, counter metrics, thresholds, and time windows.
- `north-star-metric-designer` — Design a north-star metric and surrounding metric system for a product area.
- `metric-tree-builder` — Break a metric into drivers so teams can diagnose and intervene with more precision.
- `counter-metric-guardrail-builder` — Define side-effect metrics and guardrails before pushing a primary metric.
- `instrumentation-plan-writer` — Specify events, properties, and analysis questions before launch or experiment.
- `ab-test-designer` — Design a product experiment with hypothesis, metrics, segmentation, and risks.
- `experiment-design-lite` — Create the smallest viable test when speed matters more than full rigor.
- `experiment-readout-writer` — Turn raw experiment results into a decision-grade readout.
- `retention-diagnosis-playbook` — Diagnose retention changes before jumping to random fixes.
- `activation-funnel-fixer` — Find and fix the highest-friction steps in the activation funnel.

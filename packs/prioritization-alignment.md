# Prioritization & Alignment Pack

Make trade-offs explicit, align stakeholders, and turn noise into decisions.

## Included skills

- `prioritization-tradeoff-advisor` — Recommend what to do now, what to defer, and how to explain the trade-offs.
- `stakeholder-alignment-brief` — Create a one-page brief for cross-functional alignment.
- `decision-memo-writer` — Write a structured recommendation memo with alternatives, trade-offs, and risk.
- `escalation-brief` — Compress a blocked decision into a fast executive escalation brief.
- `opportunity-solution-tree` — Map outcome to opportunity to candidate bets in a lightweight tree.
- `roadmap-theme-builder` — Group work into coherent roadmap themes instead of random line items.

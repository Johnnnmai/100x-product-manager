## English

### The 100X Philosophy

We believe Product Management should be:

1. **Market-Driven, Not Idea-Driven**
   - The market decides what succeeds, not our internal opinions
   - Every feature must pass the market validation test
   - "Does the market need this?" comes before "Can we build this?"

2. **Speed as Competitive Advantage**
   - In a fast-moving market, speed IS the strategy
   - A good product shipped fast beats a perfect product shipped late
   - Move fast, iterate faster

3. **10x Thinking Over 10% Improvements**
   - Incremental thinking leads to incremental results
   - Ask: "What would 10x growth look like?"
   - Don't optimize for 10% - optimize for 10x

4. **Learn Fast, Fail Fast, Pivot Fast**
   - Hypotheses are cheap to test
   - Fail quickly, learn quickly, pivot quickly
   - The cost of being wrong is low when you move fast

### The Problems We're Solving

Traditional PM frameworks often suffer from:

- **Analysis Paralysis**: Too much process, not enough action
- **Perfectionism**: Shipping too late, learning too slow
- **Internal Focus**: Building for ourselves, not the market
- **Incremental Thinking**: 10% improvements instead of 10x leaps

### The 100X Approach

| Traditional | 100X |
|-------------|------|
| "How do we build this?" | "Does the market need this?" |
| "What's the perfect solution?" | "What's the fastest way to validate?" |
| "Let's plan for next quarter" | "Let's validate in 2 weeks" |
| "10% growth is good" | "What's our 10x lever?" |
| "We think users want..." | "The data shows..." |

### Core Principles

1. **Market Validation First** - Always ask market need before feature development
2. **Speed Over Perfection** - Ship fast, iterate faster
3. **Data Over Opinion** - Let data drive decisions
4. **10x Over 10%** - Think exponential, not incremental
5. **Hypothesis-Driven** - Test assumptions, validate quickly

## 中文

### 100X 理念

我们相信产品管理应该是：

1. **市场驱动，而非想法驱动**
   - 市场决定什么成功，而非我们内部的想法
   - 每个功能都必须通过市场验证测试
   - "市场需要这个吗？"先于"我们能构建这个吗？"

2. **速度即竞争优势**
   - 在快速移动的市场中，速度就是战略
   - 快速发布的好产品优于延迟发布的完美产品
   - 快速行动，更快迭代

3. **10倍思维优于10%改进**
   - 渐进式思维导致渐进式结果
   - 问："10倍增长会是什么样子？"
   - 不要优化10%，要优化10倍

4. **快速学习、快速失败、快速转向**
   - 假设测试成本很低
   - 快速失败，快速学习，快速转向
   - 当你行动迅速时，犯错的成本很低

### 我们要解决的问题

传统 PM 框架常常遭受以下问题：

- **分析瘫痪**: 流程太多，行动太少
- **完美主义**: 发布太晚，学习太慢
- **内部导向**: 为自己构建，而非为市场
- **渐进式思维**: 10%改进而非10倍飞跃

### 100X 方法

| 传统 | 100X |
|------|------|
| "我们如何构建这个？" | "市场需要这个吗？" |
| "什么是完美的解决方案？" | "最快验证方式是什么？" |
| "让我们规划下季度" | "让我们2周内验证" |
| "10%增长不错了" | "我们的10倍杠杆是什么？" |
| "我们认为用户想要..." | "数据显示..." |

### 核心原则

1. **市场验证优先** - 功能开发前始终问市场需要
2. **速度优于完美** - 快速发布，更快迭代
3. **数据优于观点** - 让数据驱动决策
4. **10倍优于10%** - 指数思维，而非渐进
5. **假设驱动** - 测试假设，快速验证

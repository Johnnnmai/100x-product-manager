# Big Tech vs Startup Decision Example

## Scenario

- User has one startup offer and two large-company interview processes
- Wants faster scope growth, but has limited runway
- Learns well in messy environments

## Example Inputs

**Q1:** `2`

**Q2:** `2`

**Q3:** `2`

**Q4:** `3`

## Expected Output Shape

- Recommendation: keep startup path active, but pressure-test compensation and company quality before deciding
- Memo explains that breadth and ambiguity match the user's growth goals
- Warning highlights runway risk and the danger of treating equity like guaranteed compensation
- Next-step checklist focuses on manager quality, company health, and the real scope of ownership

# SKILL: offer-architect

## Overview
Design compelling product offers that match market signals with customer needs.

## When to Use
- After trend scout identifies opportunities
- Creating new product lines
- Repositioning existing offerings
- Testing pricing strategies

## Inputs
- Validated market signal
- Target customer segments
- Competitive landscape
- Pricing constraints

## Process

### 1. Customer-Centric Design
- Map customer jobs-to-be-done
- Identify pain points and gains
- Define value proposition

### 2. Offer Structure
- Define product features vs price tiers
- Create entry points (cash products, flagship, micro assets)
- Design upsell paths

### 3. Differentiation
- Position against alternatives
- Create unique mechanisms
- Establish proof points

## Outputs
- Offer specification document
- Pricing model
- Positioning statement
- Launch requirements

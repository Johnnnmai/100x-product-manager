# SKILL: trend-scout

## Overview
Identify emerging trends, market signals, and opportunities before competitors.

## When to Use
- Starting a new product initiative
- Validating market demand
- Finding untapped niches
- Monitoring competitor movements

## Inputs
- Target market/industry
- Time horizon (short/medium/long)
- Signal types of interest (technology, behavioral, regulatory, etc.)

## Process

### 1. Signal Detection
- Scan industry publications, social platforms, patent filings
- Identify behavioral shifts in user data
- Monitor regulatory changes
- Track competitor launches and pivots

### 2. Signal Validation
- Cross-reference multiple sources
- Assess signal strength and clarity
- Evaluate timing appropriateness

### 3. Opportunity Mapping
- Map signals to potential product opportunities
- Estimate market size and timing
- Identify first-mover advantages

## Outputs
- Trend report with confidence scores
- Opportunity shortlist
- Recommended next actions

# Market Validation Sprint / 市场验证冲刺

[English](#english) | [中文](#中文)

---

## English

### When to Use This Skill

Use this when you need to **validate a market opportunity** quickly (1 week sprint).

### Duration

**1 Week (5 days)**

### Sprint Goal

Validate if a market opportunity is worth pursuing.

### Day-by-Day Plan

---

#### Day 1: Define the Hypothesis

**Morning:**
- Define the market opportunity
- State your hypothesis clearly

**Afternoon:**
- Identify who to talk to (5-10 people)
- Set up interview schedule

**Output:**
- Hypothesis statement
- Interview list

---

#### Day 2-3: Customer Discovery

**Morning (Day 2-3):**
- Conduct 3-5 customer interviews
- Ask open-ended questions
- Listen more, talk less

**Afternoon (Day 2-3):**
- Document findings
- Look for patterns

**Output:**
- Interview notes
- Pattern identification

---

#### Day 4: Synthesis

**Morning:**
- Analyze all data
- Identify themes

**Afternoon:**
- Validate or invalidate hypothesis
- Decide: Continue/Pivot/Drop

**Output:**
- Validation decision
- Evidence summary

---

#### Day 5: Decision & Next Steps

**Morning:**
- Share findings with team
- Get alignment

**Afternoon:**
- Decide next steps
- Plan next sprint if validated

**Output:**
- Go/No-go recommendation
- Next sprint plan

---

### Sprint Outputs

| Output | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Hypothesis | Clear testable statement |
| Evidence | 5-10 customer interviews |
| Decision | Validated/Pivoted/Dropped |
| Next Steps | 2-week build or pivot |

---

### Success Criteria

**Validating means:**
- [ ] 5+ customers express clear pain
- [ ] 3+ willing to pay or use now
- [ ] Market size appears sufficient
- [ ] No major red flags

---

### Key Principle

**Speed + Evidence:**
- One week is enough to validate
- Talk to real customers
- Let data decide

---

## 中文

### 何时使用此技能

当需要**快速验证市场机会**时使用（1周冲刺）。

### 时长

**1周（5天）**

### 冲刺目标

验证市场机会是否值得追求。

### 日程计划

---

#### 第一天：定义假设

**上午：**
- 定义市场机会
- 清晰陈述假设

**下午：**
- 确定要交谈的人（5-10人）
- 安排访谈时间

**输出：**
- 假设陈述
- 访谈名单

---

#### 第二天至第三天：客户发现

**上午（第二天至第三天）：**
- 进行3-5次客户访谈
- 提问开放式问题
- 多听少说

**下午（第二天至第三天）：**
- 记录发现
- 寻找模式

**输出：**
- 访谈笔记
- 模式识别

---

#### 第四天：综合

**上午：**
- 分析所有数据
- 识别主题

**下午：**
- 验证或否定假设
- 决定：继续/转向/放弃

**输出：**
- 验证决定
- 证据摘要

---

#### 第五天：决定和下一步

**上午：**
- 与团队分享发现
- 获得共识

**下午：**
- 决定下一步
- 如果验证通过，计划下一次冲刺

**输出：**
- 推进/不推进建议
- 下次冲刺计划

---

### 冲刺输出

| 输出 | 描述 |
|------|------|
| 假设 | 清晰可测试的陈述 |
| 证据 | 5-10次客户访谈 |
| 决定 | 验证通过/转向/放弃 |
| 下一步 | 2周构建或转向 |

---

### 成功标准

**验证通过意味着：**
- [ ] 5个以上客户表达明确痛点
- [ ] 3个以上愿意付费或现在使用
- [ ] 市场规模看起来足够
- [ ] 没有重大红旗

---

### 关键原则

**速度 + 证据：**
- 一周足够验证
- 与真实客户交谈
- 让数据决定

# PM Mock Interview Workflow Example

## Scenario

- Role: Senior PM, B2B SaaS
- Stage: Hiring-manager round in 6 days
- Problem: Candidate has strong experience, but answers feel flat and too tactical

## Expected Workflow Outputs

### Phase 1
- target-role calibration note
- top evaluation dimensions: product judgment, business impact, leadership

### Phase 2
- 6-story inventory
- gap found: no good failure / recovery story

### Phase 3
- 7-question mock set
- weak scores on ownership and altitude

### Phase 4
- rewrite 3 answers to emphasize decision quality and business outcomes

### Phase 5
- 7-day practice plan with one final timed mock before interview day

- Target role: Senior Growth PM at a B2B SaaS company
- Current problem: Recruiter screens happen, but hiring-manager interviews do not
- Resume weakness: too much delivery process language, not enough growth signal

## Expected Output Shape

- Resume scorecard showing weak target clarity and business impact framing
- Top 5 fixes led by "rewrite the first 3 bullets around activation / retention outcomes"
- 3-5 rewritten bullets that replace generic PM verbs with outcome language
- Recommendation to use `pm-mock-interview-workflow` next so resume claims and interview stories match

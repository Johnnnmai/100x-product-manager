# What you get

- public signal bundle
- emerging patterns
- weak-signal list

# Common failure

Teams collect screenshots and links but never normalize them into something decision-ready.

# Best for

- PMs converting public web information into usable product signals
- founder teams doing first-pass market scanning

# 3-5 key questions

1. What category or topic is being scanned?
2. Which sources matter most?
3. What signal would change the decision?
4. What counts as noise?

# Working logic

1. Gather public evidence.
2. Normalize it into themes, repetitions, contradictions, and weak signals.
3. Separate observation from interpretation.

# Output format

- source list
- repeated signals
- contradictions
- open questions

# Review checklist

- Are observations separated from conclusions?
- Are repeated themes visible?
- Are weak signals labeled as weak?
